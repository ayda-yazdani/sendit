How members get content into the board
📤
iOS / Android share sheet Primary
Sendit appears in the native share sheet of every app that has a share button — Instagram, TikTok, YouTube, Safari, Reddit. Tap share → tap Sendit → pick board → done. Works identically across all platforms.
Same two taps regardless of which app you're in.
📋
Paste any URL Fallback
If the share sheet isn't available or someone's on desktop, they paste any video URL directly into the board. Sendit auto-detects the platform and processes it identically.
Copy link → open Sendit → paste. Three steps, any platform.
🤖
Future: auto-capture from group chat
If the group grants permission, Sendit could watch for video links shared in a WhatsApp or Telegram group and auto-add them to the board. Removes the sharing step entirely.
Not day-one — but the natural end state.
🖥️
Browser extension
A Chrome/Safari extension adding a "Send to Sendit" button on TikTok web, YouTube, Instagram web. One click from any browser, any platform.
For the group member who sends stuff from their laptop.

The one honest technical flag
The cross-platform architecture is clean and buildable. The only real complexity is that each platform has different API access rules — YouTube is the most open, TikTok is the most restricted, Instagram sits in the middle. For the MVP, start with YouTube Shorts and Instagram Reels — both have sufficient metadata access for good AI extraction. Add TikTok via third-party scraping APIs ($27/month) once the core is working. The user never sees any of this. To them it's just: share → board updates.
